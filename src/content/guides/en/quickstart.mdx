**What you just created:**

### What Syncs Automatically

Your keys work on every client. When you switch:

‚úÖ **Your posts** ‚Äî Stored on relays, available everywhere  
‚úÖ **Your followers** ‚Äî Follow list is public  
‚úÖ **Your following** ‚Äî Same as above

### What Doesn't Sync

‚ùå **App settings** ‚Äî Each client has its own preferences  
‚ùå **Drafts** ‚Äî Stored locally in each app  
‚ùå **Muted accounts** ‚Äî May vary by client  
‚ùå **Custom lists** ‚Äî Client-specific features

### How to Switch Clients Later

1. **Export your key** from current client (nsec)
2. **Download new client** or open web app
3. **Import your key** using nsec
4. **Wait for sync** ‚Äî Follows and profile load automatically
5. **Adjust settings** to your preference

## Common Questions

**"Can I use the keys I generated here on real Nostr?"**
Yes! They're real keys. Save them safely.

**"What if I mess up?"**
In simulation? No consequences. On real Nostr? Keys are permanent, but you can always create new ones.

**"Do I need Bitcoin to start?"**
No. Nostr is free. Zaps (tips) are optional.

**"What if I don't like my first client?"**
Switch! Your identity (keys) works on every client. Your followers come with you.

**"Should I use multiple clients?"**
Many users do! One for mobile, one for desktop, one for specific features.
